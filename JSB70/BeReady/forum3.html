Any way to recover quickly?

I've just recovered from the flu, but I'm still feeling weak and tired. How long does it usually take to get back to full strength?

--closed on 07/11/24--

LimpNoodle99

It can take a week or two to get back to full strength. Take it easy and don't push yourself too hard. Slowly reintroduce exercise and keep eating nutritious meals.

RecoveryRaccoon

Everyone's different, but generally, you might feel off for about 2-3 weeks. Just keep resting, staying hydrated, and eating well. And avoid getting sick again!

WobblyWombat

You might feel weak for a bit. Gradually get back into your routine, listen to your body, and don't overdo it. A little sunshine and fresh air can work wonders, too!
