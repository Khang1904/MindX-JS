Flu Activity

The CDC reports that influenza activity during the 2023-2024 season has been moderately severe, peaking in late December 2023 and remaining elevated through February 2024 before declining in May 2024.



Predominant Virus

Influenza A (H1N1)pdm09 was the most common virus, but influenza A (H3N2) and B/Victoria viruses became more prevalent later in the season

Vaccination

The CDC continues to recommend flu vaccination, especially for high-risk groups such as older adults, pregnant women, young children, and those with chronic medical conditions

Hospitalizations

Hospitals saw a significant rise in flu-related admissions, particularly affecting the elderly and those with preexisting conditions, leading to increased strain on healthcare services.
