How can I stop spreading

My whole family has come down with the flu. What's the best way to keep it from spreading to others in our household?

--closed on 11/05/22--

GerminatorX

Quarantine the sickies! Keep them in one room with their own utensils and towels. Disinfect surfaces often and make hand sanitizer your new BFF.

FluMasterFlex

Open the windows for ventilation, and have everyone wash their hands frequently. Face masks can help, too. Think of it as an opportunity to unleash your inner ninja.

SneezeGuardWarrior

Stock up on disinfectant wipes and spray everything down like you're prepping for an alien invasion. Separate sleeping arrangements and lots of hand washing are key.
